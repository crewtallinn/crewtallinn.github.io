About us

EMITO OÜ is a crewing agency founded in 2009 and headquartered in Tallinn, Estonia.
We specialize in providing crew management services to Norwegian and Danish shipowners, primarily supporting cargo vessels.
Our extensive database comprises qualified, certified, and experienced seafarers, ensuring reliable and professional staffing solutions.
We welcome your inquiries regarding marine personnel and look forward to supporting your crewing needs.

Certificates

EMITO OÜ operates in full compliance with ISO 9001 and the Maritime Labour Convention (MLC) 2006 standards.
We are certified by Bureau Veritas and the Polish Register of Shipping PRS.
